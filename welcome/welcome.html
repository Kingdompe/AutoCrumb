How AutoCrumb Works

Simple, automatic, and respectful of your workflow.

1

Browse normally

While a tab is open, its cookies are safe. No interruptions.

2

Close a tab

AutoCrumb waits 15 seconds (configurable), then checks if any other tab uses the same domain.

3

Cookies cleaned

If no tab is open for that domain and it's not whitelisted, cookies are deleted. A badge shows the count.

Pro tip: Right-click any page to quickly whitelist or greylist it. Or use Alt+Shift+W to whitelist instantly.
